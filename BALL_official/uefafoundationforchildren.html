Information and call for project proposals

 “Adopt A Ball For All” Donation Program

The goals of this Donation Program are:

  • Donation of light sound balls for children and youngsters with Visual Impairment
  • Heading for Inclusion where sighted children play together with Visual Impaired ones

Eligibility of structures and project proposals

We are open to all UN recognized countries around the world.

Focus Areas

Key performance factors to be reviewed are:

  • Age/ number and gender of the beneficiaries
  • Action Plan

Equipment

The “Adopt A Ball For All Donation Program” will provide structures with the following equipment: 10 mini blind footballs.

Financial conditions

Youthorama NGO will arrange for the equipment to be purchased and delivered to you. Organizations will not be in charge of buying or shipping the equipment.

Timeline

The call for project proposals is open from Monday March 9th, until 2021 Friday April 2nd, 2021. During this period the Youthorama team will be available to answer questions related to the grant and your application. Successful applicants will be informed of the results of the selection process no later than end of April 2021. The equipment will be purchased by May 31st, 2021 and delivered as soon as possible thereafter.

Review and selection process

The review of the submitted projects will be undertaken by Youthorama NGO. To be considered for this grant, proposals must be submitted on the official online application form and be in line with the project proposal criteria. Project proposals with missing documentation will not be eligible and will not be reviewed.

Document Checklist

If your project is selected, your structure will also be asked to provide the following documents, latest by the end of September 2021:

  • A detailed report with the results of the project
  • The “Adopt A Ball For All Donation Program” video-photo release form
  • Photos/videos of VI people playing with the equipment
  • Testimonies of beneficiaries

Submission of project proposals

Please make sure your complete project proposal reaches us online by Friday April 2nd, 2021.   Don’t hesitate to contact us for any further information via email to: orama2003@gmail.com  
Title the email with your project proposal: Application “Adopt A Ball For All Donation Program”.
